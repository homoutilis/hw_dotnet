If you want to Book Room in Hotel, you need to:
1. Choose destination. Choose check-in date. Choose check-out date. Choose number of guests.
2. Set filters.
3. Then you will have list with hotels.
4. If list is empty, you must choose another settings.
5. Else you have to read all about hotels from first to last hotel in the list.
6. If the hotel doesn't suit you, go to the next hotel.
7. If the hotel suits you, you have to read all about rooms in list of rooms of this hotel.
8. If the room doesn't suit you, go to the next room.
9. Else you begin to book this room (input your first name, last name and e-mail) and don't search again.
